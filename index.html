About The Quiz

Hi there! Welcome to my javaScript quiz. Please press "Start" to begin. Once you've started, a timer will appear in the upper right-hand corner, and a countdown will begin to display questions. Answer quickly, but be careful! Each wrong answer takes away 5 seconds from the clock. The quiz ends once your time has run out, or you've attempted all questions. Good luck!
