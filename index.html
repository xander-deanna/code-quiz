About The Quiz

Hi there! Welcome to my javaScript quiz. Just press "Start" go get started. Once you press "Start", a timer in the upper right-hand corner will begin a countdown and the quiz will begin to display questions. Answer quickly, but be careful! Each wrong answer takes away 5 seconds from the clock. The quiz ends once your time has run out. Good luck!
